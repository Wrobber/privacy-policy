## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Natural Farming Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Formulas and Data:** Any formulas, calculations, or data you input into the app are stored locally on your device only.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** We do not collect any usage statistics, analytics, or tracking data.
- **Device Information:** We do not collect device identifiers or technical information.

## How We Use Information

- All formulas and calculations are processed locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is used for advertising or marketing purposes

## Data Storage

- All app data is stored locally on your iOS device
- Data persists in the app's local storage
- Data is backed up through your device's standard iCloud backup (if enabled by you)
- We do not have access to any data stored in the app

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to function.

## Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features. Since all data remains on your device, you maintain complete control over your information.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access:** All your data is accessible within the app
- **Deletion:** You can delete any stored formulas or data within the app
- **Export:** You can export your formulas using the app's export features (if applicable)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
